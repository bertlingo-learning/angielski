Temat rozmowy

Opowiedz mi o swoim idealnym dniu. Co zwykle robisz? Co robiłeś wczoraj? A co robisz teraz, w tej chwili?

  • Present Simple dla zwyczajów: "I usually eat sushi on Fridays."
  • Past Simple dla wczoraj: "Yesterday I played Minecraft for two hours."
  • Present Continuous dla teraz: "Right now I am doing my English homework."
  • Spróbuj użyć min. 4 nowych słówek z dzisiejszych fiszek!

Did You Know? — Ciekawostki
A few fun facts in English. How many can you understand without translating?
🦖
The word "dinosaur" comes from Greek and means "terrible lizard". But most dinosaurs were actually more closely related to birds than to lizards!
🍣
Sushi doesn't mean "raw fish" — it actually refers to the vinegared rice. You can have sushi with vegetables, cooked egg, or even fruit!
✈️
A Boeing 747 has about 6 million parts. Half of them are fasteners (bolts, rivets, screws). Imagine building that in Minecraft!
⛏️
Minecraft is the best-selling video game of all time with over 300 million copies sold. The entire surface area of a Minecraft world is about 8 times the size of Earth.
🌍
There are about 1.5 billion English speakers in the world, but only around 400 million of them are native speakers. Most people learned it just like you!
